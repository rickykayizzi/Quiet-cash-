Quiet Cash — User Guide

Welcome to Quiet Cash! This guide will help you get started with tracking your money easily.

1. Adding a Balance

Enter your starting balance in the “Balance” section. This is your available money.

2. Tracking Savings

Go to the “Savings” tab to add and monitor your savings goals. You can edit or delete goals anytime.

3. Tracking Loans

In the “Loans” tab, add loans you have given or taken. Each entry shows details and updates as you repay or collect.

4. Transaction History

All your activity (savings, loans, expenses) is logged under “History.” You can review past entries anytime.

5. Reports

The “Reports” tab shows charts of your savings and loan activity. Use this to see trends over time.

6. Data Storage

Quiet Cash saves everything in your browser’s local storage. This means your data is private to your device. If you clear browser data, the app will reset.

7. Ads

You may see ads while using Quiet Cash. These help support development and keep the app free.
