Why Track Your Money?

Money disappears in the quietest ways — a 5,000 UGX airtime top-up, a 3,000 UGX boda-boda ride, a coffee bought without thought. By the end of the week, you wonder where it all went. Tracking gives you eyes where you didn’t have them, ears where you thought there was silence, and control where you assumed you had none.

Top Tips to Start Today

1. Set aside at least 10,000 UGX (~$2.60) weekly — small wins compound into security.
2. Track all expenses, even the smallest, to spot hidden leaks.
3. Carry only the cash you need — impulse has no home.
4. Set realistic goals: a phone, a TV, school fees — and measure progress with Quiet Cash.
