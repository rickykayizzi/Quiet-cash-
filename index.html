Quiet Cash is your simple, everyday tool for tracking income, expenses, and savings—whether in cash or mobile money. Stop wondering where your money goes, and start making every shilling count.

What is Quiet Cash?

Quiet Cash is designed for people who deal with both cash and mobile money daily. In many homes, money slips away quietly—on transport, snacks, airtime, and impulse buys. Our mission is to help you capture those small leaks and build habits that grow your finances over time.

With Quiet Cash, you don’t need to be a financial expert. You’ll get real-time tracking, clear reports, and simple savings tools that make money management less stressful and more rewarding. Whether you earn daily wages, run a small business, or just want better control over your budget, this is for you.

We believe money is not just about numbers—it’s about freedom, peace of mind, and future opportunities. Quiet Cash gives you the visibility and confidence to take charge of your finances, one coin at a time.
